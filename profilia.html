Applications:

Our solutions are meticulously crafted to meet diverse structural needs, offering unparalleled performance across residential, commercial, industrial, and agricultural applications. Each solution is engineered for durability, ensuring optimal protection and efficiency in all environments.

Residential: Offers robust protection against harsh weather, ensuring lasting durability for homes.

Commercial: Provides a sleek, resilient solution for high-traffic commercial spaces.

Industrial: Built to endure extreme industrial conditions with minimal maintenance.

Agricultural Buildings: Enhances thermal efficiency, maintaining optimal temperatures in agricultural settings.

Technical Specifications:

Pre-Painted Alu-ZincSteel Sheets (Galvalume*) (55% Aluminium 45% Zinc Alloy)

Alu-Zinc Coating: AZ70-150GSM
Primer service coat: 5-7 microns epoxy/PU on both sides
Painting thickness (top): 20-30 microns
Painting thickness (bottom): 5-7 microns
Surface paint reflection: Semi Gloss/Matt finish
Conforms to: IS 15965 (2012)
Yield Strength: 550 MPA
