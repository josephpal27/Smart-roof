Privacy Policy

Smart Roof is strongly committed to your right to privacy and to keeping your personal information secure. To earn your trust and confidence, we fully disclose our privacy practices. We encourage you to read this privacy statement to understand what types of personally identifiable information we collect and how we use this information. Smart Roof reserves the right to amend its privacy policy to meet the evolving needs of the website and will inform you about any updates at the earliest possible time.

How We Collect and Use Information
Smart Roof collects only the necessary personally identifiable information to respond to your requests for customized products and services. Personally identifiable information includes, but is not limited to, your first and last name, physical address, email address, phone number, interests, birth date, and other information that may identify you as a specific individual.

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you knowingly provide it. Smart Roof uses this information to operate its website and services and to inform you of new features, services, and products.

Cookies and Tracking
When you visit smartroofing.co.in, we may send one or more cookies—a small text file containing a string of alphanumeric characters—to your device that uniquely identifies your browser and helps us enhance your navigation through the site. A cookie may also provide us with information on how you use the site (e.g., the pages you view, the links you click) and allow us to track your usage over time. Persistent cookies remain on your device after you close your browser and may be used on subsequent visits. You can remove persistent cookies by following your web browser’s directions. Session cookies are temporary and disappear once you close your browser. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse all cookies, but please note that certain features of the site may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Third-Party Services
We may carefully select other companies to send you information about their products or services related to Smart Roof’s offerings. If we intend to use your information for such purposes, we will provide you with the option and instructions to decline this service. You may also opt out of receiving promotional emails from Smart Roof by following the instructions included in the emails.

Legal Disclosures
Smart Roof may disclose personally identifiable information if required to do so by law or in good faith that such action is necessary to:

Comply with legal obligations or processes.
Protect and defend the rights or property of Smart Roof, the site, or its users.
Act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users or the public.
User Interaction Features
The website may include features allowing users to disclose personal information, such as email addresses, while interacting with other members. Any disclosure of personal details through these features is at your sole risk. If you believe your privacy has been compromised, please notify us by email at info@smartroofing.co.in.

Smartroofing.co.in may contain links to other websites. Smart Roof is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such third-party sites.

Contact Us
