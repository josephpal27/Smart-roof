C & Z Purlins

The Strength That Shapes Every Structure

Smart Roof CZ Purlins are high-quality cold-formed steel structural members designed to provide strong, lightweight, and cost-effective support for roofing and wall systems.

Manufactured using high tensile galvanized steel, CZ Purlins play a critical role in modern steel structures by supporting roof sheets, cladding and structural loads with precision and durability.

Purlins are horizontal structural members that run parallel to the building roof and support the roofing sheets, ensuring stability and load distribution across the structure.

C Purlins are designed with a C-shaped cross-section and parallel flanges, offering reliable structural support for roofing and cladding systems. Ideal for shorter spans, they provide a perfect balance of strength, stability, and ease of installation.

STRONGER SPANS - SMARTER SUPPORT

Z Purlins are designed with a Z-shaped cross-section, allowing them to overlap at joints for enhanced strength and continuity. Ideal for longer spans, they provide superior load-bearing capacity and structural efficiency for large-scale projects.
